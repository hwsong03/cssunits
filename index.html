About Daniel

Daniel Arsham blurs the lines between art, architecture, and performance.

Key Milestones:

  • 1980: Born in Cleveland, Ohio.
  • 2003: Graduated from Cooper Union in New York City.
  • 2008: Presented his first solo show in New York.
  • 2013: Became known for his "Future Relic" series.
  • 2018: Collaborated with renowned brands and artists to bring his unique vision to wider audiences.

Iconic Works

Daniel's unique approach combines architecture with a play of light and shadow, often invoking feelings of surrealism. His crystalline and erosion-like sculptures give the impression of being uncovered from some long-lost civilization.
